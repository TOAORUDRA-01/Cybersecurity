Phase 1 — Foundations

Build the bedrock. Linux CLI, networking concepts, and Python scripting. Every offensive and defensive skill assumes this knowledge. Don't rush it — confusion here compounds later.

30
Days
8–10
Hrs/week

Phase 2 — Core Security

Now you understand how systems work — learn how they break. Cryptography, attack categories, vulnerability concepts, and Security+ exam preparation. This phase ends with a clear career direction.

30
Days
10
Hrs/week
WK 05 Cryptography & Authentication
freecert prep
▾
Mon + Tue
Symmetric & asymmetric encryption
AES, RSA, DH key exchange. How HTTPS actually works end-to-end. Why certificate authorities exist.
Crypto 101 (free PDF)
Wed
Hashing & digital signatures
MD5/SHA family, collision attacks, bcrypt for passwords. GPG signing. Verify a file's hash in terminal.
TryHackMe: Encryption 101
Thu + Fri
PKI, TLS, MFA
Certificate chains, CRL/OCSP, TOTP 2FA. Inspect a website's TLS cert from browser and openssl.
howhttps.works (comic)
Sat + Sun
Python: hash cracker script
Dictionary attack against MD5 hashes using SecLists rockyou.txt. Understand WHY salting matters.
SecLists (free)
Week 5 goal: Explain TLS handshake to a non-technical person. Write a hash cracker that resolves at least 50% of a short MD5 list.
~10 hrs
